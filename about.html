About Us

We provide professional instruction in the art of Shinkyokushin karate and promote the history, philosophy, and techniques of the founder of this full contact Karate style - Masutatsu Oyama. We also offer excellent children's karate programs tailored for kids as young as 3 years old as well as regular classes for older kids, teens, adults and families.
